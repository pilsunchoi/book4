Texas Special Education: Landscape & Open Data Directory#

Claude 제작#

Here’s the interactive dashboard for Texas Special Education open-source data. It’s organized into 8 tabs:

🔗 Texas Special Education: Landscape & Open Data Directory (https://claude.ai/public/artifacts/80c59c9f-c46f-4737-a5a3-febcbd6867b4)

  • Overview — Key metrics at a glance + enrollment trend & disability distribution previews

  • Enrollment — 10-year student count and rate changes from 2015-16 through 2024-25 (dual Y-axis chart)

  • Disability — All 13 IDEA disability categories shown via horizontal bar chart + donut chart

  • By Region — Sortable table of all 20 ESC regions with click-to-highlight and top-10 bar chart

  • Placement — Least Restrictive Environment (LRE) breakdown by educational setting

  • Outcomes — Graduation/dropout trend lines + SPP Indicator achievement status (Met/Not Met)

  • Demographics — Race/ethnicity comparison (SPED vs. all students) + age group distribution

  • Data Catalog — 12 open data source cards categorized by State, Federal, Nonprofit, and Research, each with a direct external link to the original dataset
